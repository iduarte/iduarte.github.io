The BioLogical Footnote

Bite size science explained from the margins.

The BioLogical Footnote is where science steps out of the main text and speaks for itself, in a simple logical way. This blog explores the curious mechanisms, hidden stories, and charming contradictions that live in the margins of scientific thinking. Each piece takes a small detail: a concept, a puzzle, a metaphor, a book, an article, a question, and gives it the space to wander, explain, argue, or just bring to light. It’s science outreach from the perspective of the a curious scientist: part archivist, part storyteller, part footnote that finally decided it had something important to say.
